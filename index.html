Freestyle Dojo

Professional Jiu Jitsu gym with expert trainers

At Freestyle our mission is to provide you and our community with world-class martial arts instruction. We do so in an environment that is welcoming, whether you are trying martial arts for the 1st time or already an advanced practitioner.

Located in the heart of Toronto’s West End, our dojo offers the best classes in a range of martial arts disciplines. Our expert instructors specialize in teaching Jiu Jitsu, Fitness & Conditioning Classes – For Both Kids & Adults.

All of our classes start with a free trial so you can try our dojo out and see if it is the right fit for you and your goals!

Freestyle Dojo is your destination for Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu in a clean, modern, and inviting environment. We believe that the right training space can significantly impact your BJJ journey, and we've created an atmosphere that fosters learning, hygiene, and a sense of community.

Please stop in and try your first class free.

At Freestyle Dojo, we keep it simple. We offer one fixed price that covers everything you need to get started on your BJJ journey. No hidden fees, no surprises—just an affordable way to learn and train. Here's what you can expect:

One Fixed Price: We offer a single monthly membership fee that grants you unlimited access to all our classes. Whether you want to train once a week or every day, our pricing remains the same.

Family Plan Price: One price for the entire family. Parents can train with their kids, couples can fight each other. Show up for our special Kids With Their Parents class, help your kids learn the value of being persistant in pursuing goals, or practice with your partner, as much as you want.

Belt Testing Fees: We believe that belt promotions should be based on your dedication and progress, not on additional fees. That's why we do not charge any belt testing fees. Your growth in BJJ is recognized and celebrated without extra costs.

Uniform Costs: As a No-Gi gym, we understand that traditional BJJ uniforms (gis) can be costly. You won't need to invest in specialized clothing when training with us. Comfortable athletic wear is all you need to get started.

Please stop in and try your first class free, and ask about ongoing specials.

About Us

Passion & Dedication

At Freestyle Dojo, we are dedicated to teaching the art of Jiu Jitsu with a focus on skill, respect, and community.

The Freestyle Dojo coaches bring their diverse martial arts backgrounds to their classes for a holistic approach to martial arts training.

Mike Romano is the head Jiu Jitsu instructor, founder and owner of Freestyle Dojo. For nearly 14 years he’s studied specifically Nogi Jiu Jitsu.

Mike has trained in the infamous 10th Planet system, lived in the dormitories of Tristar Gym and traveled all over the world learning techniques from some of the best grapplers on the planet. His goal is to provide a space where creativity and freedom is nurtured, and to pass on the knowledge gained from years of experience.

Facilities

At Freestyle Dojo, we take pride in offering top-notch facilities that cater to your comfort and well-being. Here's what sets us apart:

Above-Ground Space: Say goodbye to dimly lit basements or cramped corners. Our gym is located in a bright and spacious above-ground setting, providing a welcoming and well-ventilated space for your training.

Premium Mats: Your safety and training experience matter to us. That's why we've invested in high-quality mats that are not only comfortable but also durable. No puzzle mats falling apart here; we've got you covered.

Showers: Hygiene is essential in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. After an intense training session, you'll appreciate our on-site showers. Freshen up and feel your best before heading home or back to your day-to-day activities.

Community: Jiu Jitsu goes beyond training. At Freestyle Dojo, you're not just signing up for top-notch No-Gi Jiu-Jitsu instruction, you're becoming part of a close-knit community that extends far beyond the mats. We believe in building strong relationships, celebrating each other's progress, and enjoying life together.

We're all about fostering a warm, welcoming environment. It's not uncommon to find our members organizing extra training sessions, sharing tips, or supporting each other's goals. Our space is more than a gym; it's a hub for growth and camaraderie.

We love to have fun! Join us for our customer appreciation events like UFC watch parties, thrilling paintball outings, and lively karaoke nights. These gatherings are not just fun; they're where lifelong friendships are forged.

Here, every member matters, and together, we celebrate the journey of martial arts and life.
