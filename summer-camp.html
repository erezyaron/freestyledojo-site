Hey Parents!

Looking for an exciting, active, and fun-filled summer experience for your child? Look no further! By popular demand, we’re bringing back the Freestyle Dojo Summer Camp and it’s bigger and better than ever!

What’s Included?

This camp program, which includes Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Wrestling, Gymnastics, and Sport-Specific Training, is designed to help your child build:

  1. ✔️ coordination
  2. ✔️ confidence
  3. ✔️ self-defence knowledge
  4. ✔️ lifelong love of movement

Our experienced coaches foster a positive and inclusive environment where every camper feels like they belong. Each day is packed with games, martial arts, and playing sports!
